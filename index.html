Our Story

A DREAM NINE
YEARS IN THE MAKING

SUVIKK was never started just to sell sarees. It started with a feeling, a memory, and a dream quietly living in my heart since 2017.

Growing up, I watched my mother preserve her sarees with so much love — and even today, I still wear her 20-year-old sarees with the same pride and elegance. That's when I understood that a saree is never just fabric.

It is emotion. It is memory. It is a piece of one generation carried into another.

Life moved through studies, responsibilities, and corporate life — but this dream never left me. After spending 5 years in the corporate world building businesses for others, I finally felt it was time to invest my energy and heart into building my own.

Then in August 2024, during a simple conversation with my husband Vivek on our coffee date, I finally spoke this dream out loud. That one moment became the beginning of SUVIKK — built with belief, family and friends' support, and years of silent dreaming.

Today, SUVIKK is not just a brand for us. It is our way of preserving timeless elegance in a fast-moving world.

Our Vision

TIMELESS, EFFORTLESS,
DEEPLY PERSONAL

In a world of fast fashion, we want women — especially the new generation — to reconnect with the beauty, grace, and emotion of sarees beyond just occasions. We believe elegance should not fade with trends.

SUVIKK is for women who carry tradition with confidence and modernity with grace.

Because some things are never meant to go out of style.

What Makes SUVIKK Different

  • Sourced with Intention Every piece is thoughtfully sourced directly from weavers and handpicked with time, emotion, and care — just the way we would choose a saree for ourselves or someone we deeply love.
  • Curated, Not Mass-Produced We do not simply sell products. We curate pieces that feel personal, timeless, and worth holding onto for years.
  • Sarees That Become Memories Each saree carries softness, elegance, comfort, and a story within its threads — meant not just to be worn once, but remembered, repeated, and passed on.
